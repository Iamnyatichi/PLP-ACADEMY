Introduction

The field of "geospatial science" is concerned with applying information technology to comprehend the people, places, and processes that make up the planet. An essential component of the field is the spatial examination of physical and human factors.

About

Geographical data is gathered, analyzed, and stored using geospatial technology. It analyzes the effects of human activities while mapping geographic places using software. Digital software is used by Geographic Information Systems (GIS) to integrate datasets and maps pertaining to natural occurrences and socioeconomic trends. In order to properly understand complicated data, GIS produces layered maps. Each data point is linked to a specific place on Earth, making stacking feasible. Other types of geospatial technology are;

  • Geofencing
  • GPS
  • Remote sensing
